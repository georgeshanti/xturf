Ibeto | Excel 2018


IBeTo (Innovations for a Better Tomorrow) is the flagship event of Excel 2018, by the credence that technology holds the key to unlocking society’s most unnerving challenges. The 6th edition of IBeTo seeks to replicate and augment the immense responses received from numerous colleges all over India, bringing forward some of the most spectacular innovations that benefit society. The brightest of these ideas are recognized and provided mentorship to bring about innovative end products every year. IBeTo aspires to unite social relevance and technical innovation, to the pioneer and the populace.

What's new ?


IBeTo is tying up with organisations such as Bodhini, ANERT, EMC Kerala and KSWDC to bridge the gap between ideation and execution. The best chosen projects will be provided mentorship and funds to convert them into promising commodities that can save the lives of millions.

ibetohack


The Ibetohack is a Hackathon conducted to obtain a working prototype based on the problem statements which can further be iterated and launched into the market. In addition to the prize money, the top 3 contestants are guaranteed direct entry into the IBeTo finals. Under the eminent guidance and mentorship of experts from respective field, the contestants are assured wide exposure and essential hands on experience.

Duration: 24 Hours

Prizes worth 15k

Problem Statements:

  • Zero Carbon Revolution
  • Sustainable ENergy Conservation
  • Care for Women Health and Hygiene
  • Safe cyberspace for Children
  • Innovations and Aid in Agriculture
  • Tackling Natural Disasters
